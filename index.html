InstaScroll

Scroll Instagram with your volume buttons.

Download APK

v1.0 · Android 8+

🔼

Volume Up — Previous Post

Press Vol Up to scroll back to the previous post.

🔽

Volume Down — Next Post

Press Vol Down to scroll to the next post.

❤️

Double Press Down — Like

Quick double-press Vol Down to like the current post.

💬

Hold Volume Up — Comments

Hold Vol Up to open comments. Use Vol Down to scroll through them. Hold Vol Up again to close.

Setup

1

Download and install the APK on your Android device.

2

Open InstaScroll and tap Open Accessibility Settings.

3

Find InstaScroll in the list and enable it.

4

Open Instagram. Volume keys are now remapped — only while Instagram is in the foreground.
